About us

Direct Sciences Network is an initiative by multiple partners to provide a free access to scientific work. Our struggle is transfer research to knowledge that results to innovation and services. This platform offers you good quality scientific work, collaborations and networking opportunity, training, consultancy and other services through its supporting organizations. Our expert team along with reviewer cooperation makes it possible to make fast track decision on any articles for publication and/or presentation.
