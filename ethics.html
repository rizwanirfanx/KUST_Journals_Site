Ethics of Publication

All the articles are checked for plagiarism analysis and only non-plagiarized articles are forwarded to reviewer, through editorial office. Publications ethics are our core values. For any publication following information must be provided to ensure the value of publication ethics;

Funding of the Research Work

The reader has the right to know the funding source of the research conducted, so the funding source must be mentioned and acknowledged properly.

Support of the Research Work

In addition to funding source, further support (if any) for research work should also be properly acknowledged.

Duplicate Submission / Publication of the Research Work

The research work submitted to other journals should be withdrawn before submitting it to our journals. No research work will be accepted to be published, if already published. To ensure this policy, all the articles are checked through plagiarism detection software.

Authorship of the Research Work

The number and sequence of authors should be on merit and defendable.

Details of Research Ethics

For further details on research ethics, please visit the website of COPE (Committee On Publication Ethics); http://www.publicationethics.org/
